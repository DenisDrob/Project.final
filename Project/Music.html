Slipknot

Few words about this group:

Slipknot is an American heavy metal band known for their intense and aggressive sound. Formed in 1995, the band gained significant recognition for their energetic live performances, distinctive masks, and chaotic stage presence. Their music combines elements of various genres, including metal, nu metal, and alternative metal, resulting in a unique and powerful sonic experience. With their heavy riffs, pounding drums, and intense lyrics, Slipknot has amassed a dedicated fanbase and has become one of the most influential metal bands of the 21st century.
